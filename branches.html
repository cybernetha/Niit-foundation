Our Branches In Karnataka

NIIT Foundation has established several centers in Karnataka to provide skill development and vocational training, aiming to enhance employability among youth, particularly from underprivileged backgrounds. Here are some of the key centers in Karnataka:

  • NIIT Foundation – Yelahanka Career Development Center

Yelahanka, Bengaluru, Karnataka

Offers a range of courses including digital literacy, financial literacy, and professional edge programs to bridge the skill gap in the region. 

  • NIIT Foundation – IFF Tumkur Center

Tumkur, Karnataka

Provides vocational training and placement support, focusing on employment opportunities in the organized industry. 

  • NIIT Foundation – Vijayanagar Center

Vijayanagar, Bengaluru, Karnataka

Offers courses in diverse domains tailored to college students, graduates, and working professionals.
